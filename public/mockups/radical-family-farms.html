Our Story

Mixed-Asian American heritage, rooted in Sonoma soil.

Radical Family Farms grows at the intersection of cultural heritage and regenerative agriculture — cultivating the vegetables, herbs, and flavors that shaped our family's table and reflect the Asian-American communities often absent from Sonoma County's farm-to-table scene.

We farm radically not in opposition, but in deep rootedness — to our ancestors, our land, and our neighbors. Certified Organic, No-Till, No-Spray. The radical way.
