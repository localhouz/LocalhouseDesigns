# Prescribed Burn Sauces

Product brand site with stronger schema and product structure, but also meaningful competition from third-party listings, trademark pages, and legacy pages.

## Current Known Work

- richer structured data
- cleaner measurement setup
- stronger product and brand presentation

## Key EO Questions
