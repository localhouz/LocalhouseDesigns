Who we are

Small studio.
Big output.

Localhouse Designs is a boutique web development studio that lives in two worlds: modern Angular builds for consumer and brand clients, and enterprise integrations for teams running on Business Central, SAP, or Infor SyteLine.

That ERP background isn't a side note - it's the reason we understand operational complexity, know what floor-level users actually need, and can build tooling that fits inside the reality of how manufacturing and supply chain teams work.

Based in Broken Arrow, Oklahoma, the studio works across the Tulsa market, the wider state, and remote engagements that have reached as far as Sacramento, California. The site and the writing are led by Steven Rausch through Localhouse Designs.
