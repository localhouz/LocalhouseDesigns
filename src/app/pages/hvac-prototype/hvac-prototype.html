FAQs that actually help

Do you offer same-day HVAC repair in Tulsa?

Yes, the prototype assumes same-day repair positioning is one of the highest-intent entry points and should be visible immediately.

Do you finance new HVAC systems?

For replacement traffic, financing belongs near system options and estimate CTAs, not buried on a separate page.

What areas do you serve?

The service area should be named in plain English with city references, not implied through a footer-only address.
